## Data → Intelligence Pipeline
1. Aggregate all historical conversations, notes, role case studies into structured JSONL.
2. Segment into Observation → Interpretation → Intervention → Outcome units.
3. Label intents, pattern archetypes, emotional tone, risk tier, expected impact.
4. Generate temporal and affective features; embed text and activity sequences.
5. Fine-tune LLM on labeled examples for insight narration & recommendation.
6. Train lightweight on-device models for sequence detection and emotion inference.
7. Build RAG index from documentation, neuroscience references, product rules.
8. Run offline RL from historical outcomes for intervention policies.
9. Validate via replay simulations, human review, privacy/safety audits.
10. Deploy with federated learning updates, telemetry dashboards, rollback controls.

- **Persona Simulation**: Build synthetic behavior traces and decision trees from the developer/accountant/designer cases to train policy models and evaluate interventions.
- **Pattern Libraries**: Extract recurring workflow loops, emotional states, and suggested automations to seed the Action Synthesizer’s macro generators and surprise-value features.

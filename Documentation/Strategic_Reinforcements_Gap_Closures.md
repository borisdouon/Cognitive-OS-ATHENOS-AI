   - Document minimum viable telemetry (app usage, focus switches, manual annotations) and ship instrumentation toolkits alongside alpha releases.

   - Publish a data posture memo covering on-device defaults, encryption standards, lawful requests policy, and user-owned deletion controls.

   - Establish risk tiers (informational, assistive, autonomous) with escalating review, sandbox checks, and rollback tooling; enforce that high-risk automations stay recommendation-only until confidence and human approvals meet thresholds.

   - Anchor go-to-market on a flagship persona (e.g., senior developer) to concentrate data collection, success stories, and feature polish before expanding to adjacent roles.

7. **Resourcing & Technical Pragmatism**
   - Phase infrastructure ambitions: start with managed vector DB + observability SaaS; defer heavy self-hosted stacks (Milvus, Temporal, multi-region orchestration) until user scale demands it.

10. **Governance & Continuous Ethics**
    - Form an ethics and safety board with external advisors to audit models, guardrails, and emergent behaviors biannually.
